Buyback Mechanism

The buyback system functions as a long-term stabilizer and liquidity booster:

  1. The team wallet periodically purchases $NIX directly from the market using treasury funds.
  2. These tokens are added back into liquidity pools, increasing depth and reducing volatility.
  3. Alternatively, through community voting, purchased tokens may be sent to a burn address, effectively reducing total circulating supply.

This hybrid approach ensures flexibility while empowering the community to decide the best outcome ‚Äî whether growth or deflation.
